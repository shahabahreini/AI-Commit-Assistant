# Perplexity
> Verified against GitMind `5.0.2` on June 7, 2026.

Create a key in [Perplexity settings](https://www.perplexity.ai/settings/api). The default is `gpt-5.5-computer`; model discovery reflects the provider catalog. GitMind sends the selected diff and prompt to Perplexity, and some models may use online retrieval. Review the selected model's behavior and account quota.
