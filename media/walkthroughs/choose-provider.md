# Choose your AI provider

GitMind generates commit messages using the AI provider **you** choose — bring your own key, run a local model, or reuse your GitHub Copilot subscription.

**15+ providers supported**, including:

- **OpenAI** — GPT‑4o, GPT‑4
- **Anthropic** — Claude Sonnet, Claude Opus
- **Google Gemini** — generous free tier
- **DeepSeek**, **Mistral**, **Cohere**, **Perplexity**, **xAI Grok**
- **Together AI**, **OpenRouter**, **Hugging Face** (aggregators)
- **Ollama** — fully local & free
- **GitHub Copilot** — uses your existing subscription

Open **Settings** and pick a provider under *Model Settings* to get started.
